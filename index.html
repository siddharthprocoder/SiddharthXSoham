How to play

Enjoy your creative

🧭

Explore

Explore the whole world with nothing but a sword as you travel beyond horizons.

⛏️

Mine

Mine the caves and gather the resources to get stronger and build better gear.

⚒️

Craft

Craft using the materials you mined and craft better gear by choice.

🛡️

Survive

Survive because you only have 1 life and losing it can end your adventure.

🐾

Hunt

Hunt animals to get food and stay alive or die by your own choice.

🏗️

Build

Build houses and many more things to protect from danger and survive.

✦

Made with joy by two noobs who love pixelated adventures.
